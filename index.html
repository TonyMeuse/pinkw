About Us

Welcome to Pink W Firewood and Woodlands, your trusted source for premium choice grade firewood in Southwest Nova Scotia. We specialize in processing and delivering high-quality firewood that keeps your home warm and cozy throughout the cold months.

With expertise in forestry and logging, we understand the importance of properly seasoned, clean-burning wood. Proudly serving Clare, Yarmouth, Digby, and surrounding communities, our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for homeowners across the region.
